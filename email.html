Dear Bob Furzer,

CASA are trialling digital licencing for RePL holders with compatible Apple iPhone devices. This trial is similar to how you can access digital boarding passes for airlines and flights.

If you wish to trial the RePL digital licence, please login to myCASA on your Apple iPhone and complete the following steps:

  1. View your RePL from the myCASA dashboard
  2. Select "Add to Apple Wallet" on the RePL page
  3. Your RePL will then be available in the Apple Wallet of your device

You can learn more about this trial on the CASA website including when other devices will become suported.
